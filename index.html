About our home

This JW-friendly lakeside home in the charming Greenwood Park Neighborhood of Greenwood Lake, Warwick, New York, sleeps 6 and offers a comfortable, spacious retreat. It's a smoke-free, pet-free environment, and children are welcome. Relax on the porch while enjoying a view of the lake. You can enjoy the public beach, water sports rentals, and local homemade ice cream. JW couple reside on-site and are available to assist with any questions during your stay.
